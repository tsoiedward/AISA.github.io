AISA

A non-profit focused on reducing AI risks by enhancing safety and governance in Asia. It connects junior researchers and civil servants from the Global South with senior AI safety experts from the Global North.

  • Get Started

Why AISA?

  • Southeast Asia lags behind in AI preparedness, creating an "algorithmic divide" that leaves the region vulnerable to AI's adverse impacts and missing out on its benefits.
  • Despite significant investments from the U.S. and China in Southeast Asian AI startups, local capacity and governance remain weak.
  • AISA addresses this gap by developing local talent and creating region-specific AI governance frameworks.
  • Learn More

Our Goals

Enhance AI Safety Governance: Build local expertise and foster collaboration to produce practical AI safety policies.

Upskill Civil Servants: Equip mid-career civil servants with AI safety knowledge and tools to inform policymaking.

Incubate Junior Researchers: Develop junior policy researchers' skills through mentorship and practical policy input.

Foster Collaboration: Encourage cross-disciplinary and cross-regional collaboration to generate Asia-focused AI safety research.
